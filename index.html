Hackroulette

Tuesday, 3 - 4 pm, Sunset Lounge 2

Got a neat open source tool, script, hack that makes your work easier, faster, more reproducible or simply fun? In this show and tell session, give us a demo (max 5-10 minutes depending on how many submissions we get) and then send a pull-request (https://github.com/karthik/dse-hacks) with links (and ideally notes!) to a repo that we can share with others in the DSE and beyond.

Submit your idea here: http://inundata.org/t/dse
